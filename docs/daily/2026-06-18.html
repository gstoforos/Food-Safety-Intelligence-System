FDA · United StatesTIER 1
Clover Hill Dairy, LLC.
EXPANDED RECALL — all Clover Hill Dairy brand cheese (hard and soft). Includes: Soft Cuajada in Brine (5-gal bucket 30-lb, 2-gal bucket 14-lb); Soft Cuajada vacuum sealed (5-lb bags, 2.5-lb clamshell, 0.90-lb / 14-oz clamshell); Cuajada vacuum sealed (30-lb boxes); Ricotta (5-gal bucket 40-lb, 2-gal bucket 18-lb, 2.5-lb tubs, 1-lb clamshell); Soft Cuajada Crumbs; Mild Cheese Varieties — Yummy Cheddar, White Cheddar, White Colby, Monterey Jack, Marble Jack (½-lb, 1-lb, 2.5-lb, 5-lb, 40-lb); Sharp Cheese Varieties — Cheddar, Yummy Cheddar, White Cheddar, Monterey Jack; Flavored — Horseradish, Old Bay Cheddar; Pepper Jack Varieties — Jalapeño Cheddar, Pepper Jack, Sizzlin Colby with Habanero Peppers; Smoked Cheddar (Cheddar, White Cheddar); Smoked Pepper — Jalapeño Cheddar; plus Fresh Cheddar Curd and assorted Snack Pack / 3-in-a-Pack stix formats. Plant permit number 24-128 printed on clamshell containers.
Listeria monocytogenes — FDA Company Announcement + Publish Date both 2026-06-18. EXPANSION of the earlier Clover Hill Dairy recall (R57, 2026-06-09) which covered only Soft Ricotta/Requesón cheese. The expansion broadens scope to ALL Clover Hill Dairy brand cheese due to potential Listeria monocytogenes contamination. As of 2026-06-18, the outbreak count linked to soft cheese products stands at 9 illnesses, 8 hospitalizations, and 1 death. Distribution: direct retail market, farmers markets, and third-party distributors across NC, NY, VA, MD, NJ, and Washington DC. Products may be relabeled under brands KESSO, QUESOS LA RICURA, IZALCO, DE MI PUEBLO, and RIO LINDO. Consumers urged to check manufacturer permit number 24-128 on clamshell containers.
