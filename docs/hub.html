What is the Food Safety Intelligence System?
🧫
Pathogen-Focused Monitoring
We track only pathogen-related recalls — Salmonella, Listeria monocytogenes, E. coli O157:H7, and other biological hazards. Allergen, label, and foreign material incidents are excluded.
🌍
Global Regulatory Coverage
Data cross-referenced monthly from FDA (USA), EFSA/RASFF (EU), FSA UK, CFIA (Canada), and FSANZ (Australia/New Zealand).
✦
AI-Enriched Intelligence
Each recall validated and enriched using AI — normalising pathogen strain names, verifying geographic data, repairing source links, and ranking by severity.
📊
Monthly Ranked Reports
Every month: a curated Top 10 recall list, full pathogen breakdown, regional statistics, and trend analysis for food safety professionals worldwide.
